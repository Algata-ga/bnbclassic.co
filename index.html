SMART CONTRACT

TOKENOMICS

BNB Classic (BNBC) is the step forward in yield-generating contracts on the Binance Smart Chain (BSC); you receive rewards distributed in BUSD rather than token reflections and the contract employs a static reward system. This allows rewards to extend outside of simply holding our coin as would a reflection-based coin operate. When buying and holding a percent of the supply, every 60 minutes a dividend tracker will automatically calculate and distribute dividends among holders with your respective amount directly deposited within your wallet holding BNBC. There are no actions required in order to have this work and there is only a minimum amount of 1M Tokens required to hold in order to receive your rewards. *The dividends are distributed as Binance Peg-BUSD tokens. There are no rewards reflected back into BNBC, thus your BNBC amount held will be unaffected.
